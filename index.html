Facebook Content Monetization Guide

Facebook Content Monetization allows creators to earn money from their original videos, reels and content. Your page must follow Facebook Partner Monetization Policies and Community Standards.

Eligibility

  • Professional Facebook Page
  • Original Content
  • No Copyright Issues
  • Community Standards Follow
    • Partner Monetization Policies Follow

How To Apply

  • Open Facebook Professional Dashboard.
  • Click Monetization.
  • Check Eligibility.
    • Complete Identity Verification.
  • Add Bank Account.
  • Add Tax Information (if required).
  • Click Apply.

    • Requirements

    • Original Videos
    • Original Reels
    • Active Audience
    • Good Engagement
    • No Fake Views
    • No Reused Content
    • No Community Violations

    Tips For Fast Approval

    • Upload HD Videos.
    • Post Daily.
    • Create Original Content.
    • Avoid Copyright Music.
    • Follow Facebook Rules.
    • Use High Quality Thumbnails.

    Required Documents

    • Government ID
    • Bank Account
    • Tax Information (If Needed)
    • Email Address
